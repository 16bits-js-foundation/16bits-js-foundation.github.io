About the 16bits JS Foundation

The 16bits JS Foundation is a group of open source Web developer's, who are interested in retrocomputing, who works on large and small projects.

Our mission is to have fun, build projects made with web technologies, and JavaScript and help grow the JavaScript ecosystem as a whole.
